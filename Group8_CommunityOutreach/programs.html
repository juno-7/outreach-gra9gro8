Serving Smiles - Canteen Appreciation Day

Made Possible By 9 St. Matthew

A heartfelt celebration to recognize the dedication of our canteen staff. Thank you for serving us with love and care!

This outreach event is designed to bring people together through fun, connection, and shared experiences. The program kicks off with an exciting game of "Eh Ikaw?", where participants quickly respond to questions, encouraging quick thinking and lively interaction. Following the game, we’ll have an intermission period featuring performances from our talented classmates, offering a chance for everyone to enjoy some amazing local talent. The event wraps up with a meal time, where coworkers and workers sit down together to share a delicious meal, fostering camaraderie and community. It’s an engaging mix of entertainment, talent, and meaningful connections, ensuring that everyone leaves with smiles and memories.

As the day unfolds, the energy continues to build, creating an atmosphere of unity and enjoyment. The games and performances offer moments of laughter and celebration, while the meal time provides a chance to relax, bond, and strengthen relationships. This event isn’t just about entertainment—it’s about building a sense of belonging and creating memories that last. Everyone is encouraged to participate, whether through joining the game, supporting the performers, or simply enjoying the time spent together. By the end of the day, the goal is to leave everyone feeling connected, uplifted, and part of something special, with a renewed sense of community and friendship.
