Serving Smiles - Canteen Appreciation Day

Made Possible By 9 St. Matthew

A heartfelt celebration to recognize the dedication of our canteen staff. Thank you for serving us with love and care!

By offering a warm meal and a break, we hope to create an atmosphere of gratitude and encouragement. This event not only nourishes the body but also serves as a reminder of the importance of kindness and support in our daily lives. Through this initiative, we aim to spread joy and appreciation, making sure the hardworking members of our community feel valued and uplifted.

Our outreach event, "Serving Smiles - Canteen Appreciation Day," is all about showing appreciation for the hardworking individuals who often go unnoticed. During this event, we take a moment to serve delicious meals to the workers, providing them with a much-needed break from their busy schedules. This day is dedicated to giving back to those who tirelessly contribute to our community, ensuring that they get the recognition and rest they truly deserve.
